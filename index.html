Clear positive corr between tooth & dose

g1 <- ggplot(ToothGrowth, aes(x=factor(dose),y=len,fill=factor(dose)))

Confidence intervals and/or hypothesis tests

fit <- lm(len ~ dose + supp, data=ToothGrowth)
confint(fit)
